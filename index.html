Guidelines

1 There is a monster hiding behind one of these doors.
2 Your job will be to open all the doors, except the one where the monster is hidden.
3 Click on the doors to open it.
4 If the monster catches you, it will be GAME OVER!
5 When you click New Game, the game will be restarted, resetting longest streak at 0.
6 Can you keep the longest streak?
